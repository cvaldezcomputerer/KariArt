How can individuals with limited culinary and organizational skills be motivated to cook more at home?

In this project, I built a meal keeper mobile app concept to assist students who are frustrated by their inability to save money because of excessive spending on dining out.

Personalization

When users have uploaded their recipes, the AI aspect
will tailor the recipe’s based on user preferences they
have set. For example, if a meal is too costly, the
ingredients can be replaced with a simpler and cheaper
option. Or if the user would like to add meat to their
meal a suggestion can be given.

Creating Meal Plan

Three meals are automatically suggested to the user, but
they have the option to expand their recipe options if
the suggestions aren’t good enough.

A little bit of conversational help

No matter what time users go to the grocery store,
chatbot support will be available 24/7. Users can get
help with quick ingredient substitutions and alternate
meal suggestions.

What can be done differently?

• Time constraints: Due to the specific deadline of the masters project, I wasn’t
able to do much user testings after completing a prototype.

• Artificial Intelligence: I would’ve liked to spend more time exploring this
aspect of the mobile app because I would like to see how integrating AI
enhances the user experience.
