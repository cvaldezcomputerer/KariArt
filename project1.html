Meet Buddy

A mobile app to help relieve exam induced
stress and anxiety by using mixed reality.

Buddy was created based on real university students struggles.

Building the app began by gathering feedback from students on campus.
We conducted surveys that questioned the students study habits and
school background.

Contexts of use

To precisely define our target audience, contexts of use were created. Research on students gave feedback about their most common study spaces, tech habits, goals and motivations, and study behaviors.

Goals

  • Help students manage exam stress
  • Encourage healthy study routines
  • Remind about pauses in between
  • Relieve exam stress

Gamification: Ilustrating game cards and creating incentives.

From user feedback, it was discovered that students want an app that
feels similar to a game and doesn’t represent reality. The screens are designed with a game like interface.

What can be done differently?

• Time constraints: Due to the specific deadline of the masters project, we
weren’t able to further test the concept and reiterate features and the design.

• User testing limitations: We would’ve liked to dedicate a month towards a
user using the concept of the app by recording how they feel in a diary. This
way we can identify weaknesses and see if it stands out from existing apps.
